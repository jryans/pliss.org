FAQ

Frequently asked questions

  • When does the school start and end?

    We start on Sun May 9th at 13:30 and end on Fri 15th at 12:00. Students should have arrived in Bertinoro before 13:00 on Sunday at the latest and are expected to stay the whole week.

  • What is covered by the registration costs?

    Accommodation for the whole week in shared rooms, breakfast and lunch as well as an excursion on Wednesday. Students will only have to pay for the cost of travel to Bertinoro and for their dinners in the many cosy restaurants in the town.

  • Can I get support to attend the Summer School?

    If you would be unable to attend without financial assistance, then let us know: we have various funds available to support deserving students. We do require those to whom we offer assistance to be considerate and modest as our funds are not unlimited and different sources of funding come with different limitations. However, we will do our best to support you in attending PLISS. If you are accepted for PLISS, check with the organizers before booking any travel.

  • Who can attend?

    PLISS 2020 is aimed at mid/late stage PhD students and early postdocs working in and around programming languages. We will accept some applicants from other career stages, though PLISS 2021 will be aimed at more junior applicants. We are particularly interested in encouraging minorities and also undergraduates. Anyone else is welcome space permitting. Note: previous attendees of PLISS are eligible to attend PLISS 2020.

  • How to apply?

    Registration hasn't opened yet. Please check back later for more details. Don't hesitate to ask questions if you have any!

  • Are there prerequisites?

    An undergraduate programming language class and/or compiler class is all you need.
