FAQ

Frequently asked questions

  • When does the school start and end?

    We start Sun May 9th at 13:30 and end on Fri 15th at 12:00. Students should have arrived in Bertinoro before 13:00 on Sunday at the latest and are expected to stay the whole week.

  • What is covered by the registration costs?

    Accommodation for the whole week in shared rooms, breakfast and lunch as well as an excursion on Wednesday. Students will only have to pay for the cost of travel to Bertinoro and for their dinners in the many cosy restaurants in the town.

  • Can I get support to attend the Summer School?

    Yes, we have various funds available to support deserving students! Different sources of funding come with different limitations, but we will do our best to support you. Please be modest in your requests and try to save money wherever possible. If you are accepted for PLISS, check with the organizers before booking any travel.

  • Who can attend?

    The main target audience are students interested in programming languages. We are particularly interested in encouraging minorities and also undergraduates. Anyone else is welcome space permitting. Note: we won't accept previous attendees of PLISS, as we want to maximise the number of people who can enjoy the PLISS experience.

  • How to apply?

    Registration hasn't opened yet. Please check back later for more details. Don't hesitate to ask questions if you have any!

  • Are there prerequisites?

    An undergraduate programming language class and/or compiler class is all you need.
