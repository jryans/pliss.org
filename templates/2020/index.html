Bertinoro, Italy

May 9 to 15, 2020

The Third International Programming Language Implementation Summer School (PLISS) will be held in Bertinoro, Italy from May 9 to 15, 2020.

The Summer School's goal is to prepare PhD students for advanced study in the field. This will be done through a combination of lectures on language implementation techniques and short talks exploring the state of the art in programming language research and practice.
